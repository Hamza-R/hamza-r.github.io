Bootcamp 2020: Ball Chasing Game

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of loops,groups and arrays. I created seperate classes for all my entities, my player and enemy balls which stored their basic characterisitscs such as size, speed and starting coordinates. Although for the enemy balls I used random to decide where they would initially appear on the screen. The aim of the game is to eat all the balls as quickly as you can. However at the end I was unable to find a way to remove a ball from the enemy ball array after it collided with the player.
